Aloo Gobi

Aloo Gobi is a classic North Indian dish made with potatoes, cauliflower, and a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients

For the Curry:

  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp dry mango powder (amchur)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add potatoes and mix well. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add cauliflower florets, mix, and cover again. Cook for another 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and dry mango powder. Mix well.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
