Poha

Poha is a light and flavorful Indian breakfast dish made with flattened rice, onions, and mild spices. This quick and easy recipe is perfect for a healthy start to your day!

Ingredients

For the Poha:

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small potato, diced (optional)
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 lemon (juice)
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the poha under running water for a few seconds until it softens. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add cumin seeds, peanuts, and curry leaves. Sauté until the peanuts turn golden.
  4. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and diced potatoes (if using). Sauté until onions turn soft and potatoes are cooked.
  5. Sprinkle turmeric powder and mix well.
  6. Add the soaked poha, salt, and gently mix everything together.
  7. Cover and let it cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Turn off the heat and squeeze lemon juice over the poha.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
