Filter Coffee

Filter coffee, also known as South Indian coffee, is a rich and aromatic coffee made using a traditional coffee filter. Follow this recipe to make authentic filter coffee at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground filter coffee powder
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/2 cup boiled milk
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. Add coffee powder to the upper chamber of a traditional South Indian coffee filter.
  2. Pour hot water over the coffee powder and cover it. Allow the decoction to drip down slowly into the lower chamber.
  3. Once the decoction is ready, heat milk in a saucepan and add sugar as per taste.
  4. Pour the decoction into a cup, then add the hot milk. Adjust the strength by varying the ratio of decoction to milk.
  5. Mix well by pouring the coffee back and forth between two cups to create froth.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade filter coffee!
