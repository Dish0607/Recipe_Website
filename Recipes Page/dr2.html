Cold Coffee

Cold coffee is a refreshing and creamy beverage made with coffee, milk, and ice. Follow this recipe to prepare a delicious glass of chilled cold coffee!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp instant coffee powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • 2 tbsp water (hot)
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • Chocolate syrup (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water. Stir until the coffee dissolves completely.
  2. In a blender, add the dissolved coffee mixture, chilled milk, and ice cubes.
  3. Blend for 30-40 seconds until smooth and frothy.
  4. For extra creaminess, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and blend again.
  5. Drizzle chocolate syrup inside the serving glass (optional) and pour the blended coffee into the glass.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade cold coffee!
