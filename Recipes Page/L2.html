Bisi Bele Bath

Bisi Bele Bath is a traditional Karnataka dish made with lentils, rice, and a unique blend of spices.

Ingredients

For the Base:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 5 cups water

For the Masala:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 dried red chili
  • 1 tbsp Bisi Bele Bath powder
  • Tamarind pulp (from 1 lemon-sized ball)
  • 1 tbsp jaggery
  • Salt, to taste
  • Curry leaves & coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Instructions

  1. **Cook Rice & Dal:** Wash rice and toor dal together. Add them to a pressure cooker with **5 cups of water**, turmeric, and a little salt. Pressure cook for **4-5 whistles** until soft.
  2. **Prepare Tamarind Extract:** Soak tamarind in **1/2 cup warm water** for 10 minutes. Squeeze out the juice and discard the pulp.
  3. **Prepare Masala:** Heat **1 tbsp oil** in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chili, and curry leaves. Sauté for **30 seconds**.
  4. **Add Spice Mix:** Stir in **Bisi Bele Bath powder** and mix well.
  5. **Combine Everything:** Pour the tamarind extract into the masala. Add jaggery and salt. Cook for **5 minutes** until flavors blend.
  6. **Mix with Dal & Rice:** Pour the prepared masala into the cooked rice-dal mixture. Stir well and simmer for **5 minutes**.
  7. **Final Touch:** Drizzle **2 tbsp ghee** over the dish. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  8. **Serve Hot:** Serve with fried papad or boondi for an authentic taste.
