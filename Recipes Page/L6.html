Chicken Gravy

Chicken Gravy is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with tender chicken cooked in a rich, spiced tomato and onion-based gravy.

Ingredients

For the Gravy:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/2 cup curd or yogurt
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  6. Add curd/yogurt and stir well to avoid curdling.
  7. Add chicken pieces and coat them in the masala. Cook for 10 minutes.
  8. Pour 1 cup of water, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and kasuri methi. Mix well.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
