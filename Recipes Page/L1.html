Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and delicious rice dish layered with marinated chicken and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 cups water or chicken broth
  • Whole spices (1 bay leaf, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 cardamoms, 3 cloves)
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. **Marinate the Chicken:** In a bowl, mix chicken with yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, and ginger-garlic paste. Let it marinate for at least **1 hour** (overnight for best results).
  2. **Prepare the Rice:** Wash the basmati rice in water 2-3 times until clear, then soak it for **30 minutes**.
  3. **Cook the Chicken:** Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Add whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves) and sauté for **30 seconds** until aromatic.
  4. **Caramelize the Onions:** Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown (about **8-10 minutes**). Stir occasionally.
  5. **Cook the Masala:** Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Then, add the marinated chicken and cook on medium heat for **10-15 minutes** until the oil separates.
  6. **Add Rice & Liquid:** Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Gently mix, then pour in **2 cups of water or chicken broth**. Add salt as needed.
  7. **Slow Cook the Biryani:** Cover the pot with a lid and cook on low heat for **15-20 minutes** until rice is fully cooked and chicken is tender.
  8. **Garnish & Serve:** Sprinkle fresh coriander and mint leaves. Fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve hot with raita or boiled eggs.
