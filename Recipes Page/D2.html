Mysore Pak

Mysore Pak is a famous South Indian sweet made with gram flour, sugar, and ghee. It has a rich, melt-in-the-mouth texture and is a favorite during festivals and special occasions!

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Grease a tray or plate with ghee and set aside.
  2. Heat a pan and dry roast the gram flour for a few minutes until aromatic. Sift it to remove lumps.
  3. In another pan, heat sugar and water. Stir until the sugar dissolves and forms a one-string consistency syrup.
  4. Gradually add the roasted gram flour while stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  5. Slowly pour in the melted ghee, stirring constantly. Continue cooking until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  6. Transfer the mixture to the greased tray and spread evenly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes, then cut into desired shapes.
  8. Allow Mysore Pak to set completely before serving. Enjoy!
