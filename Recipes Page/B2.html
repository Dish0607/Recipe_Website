Ven Pongal

Ven Pongal is a comforting South Indian dish made with rice and lentils, flavored with ghee, black pepper, cumin, and cashews. Follow this simple recipe to prepare delicious Pongal at home!

Ingredients

For the Pongal:

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup yellow moong dal
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

For the Tempering:

  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp black pepper (coarsely crushed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 10-12 cashews
  • A few curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and moong dal thoroughly under running water.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the rinsed rice, moong dal, water, turmeric powder, and salt.
  3. Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles until the mixture becomes soft and mushy.
  4. In a separate pan, heat ghee and add black pepper, cumin seeds, chopped ginger, cashews, and curry leaves.
  5. Sauté until the cashews turn golden brown and the spices release their aroma.
  6. Pour the tempering over the cooked rice and dal mixture.
  7. Mix well and serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
