Fruit Smoothie

A fruit smoothie is a refreshing and nutritious drink made with fresh fruits, yogurt, and milk. Follow this simple recipe to make a delicious homemade smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruits (banana, berries, mango, or any fruit of choice)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (plain or flavored)
  • 1/2 cup milk or orange juice
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • Chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional, for extra nutrition)
  • Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the fresh fruits into small pieces for easy blending.
  2. In a blender, combine the chopped fruits, yogurt, milk (or juice), honey, and ice cubes.
  3. Blend the mixture on high speed for about 40-50 seconds or until smooth and creamy.
  4. If the smoothie is too thick, add more milk or juice and blend again until desired consistency is achieved.
  5. Pour the smoothie into serving glasses. Optionally, sprinkle chia seeds or flaxseeds on top and garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  6. Enjoy the smoothie fresh and chilled for the best taste and nutrition!
