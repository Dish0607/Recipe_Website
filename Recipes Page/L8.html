Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a classic Kashmiri curry made with tender meat slow-cooked in a flavorful blend of spices and yogurt.

Ingredients

For the Curry:

  • 500g mutton or lamb, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp fennel powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamoms
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamoms, and cinnamon. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add mutton or lamb pieces and sear until browned.
  5. Mix in yogurt, stirring continuously to avoid curdling.
  6. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, fennel powder, and salt.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 45-60 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the meat is tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
