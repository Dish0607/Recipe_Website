Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with paneer cubes cooked in a tomato-based gravy with butter and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

For the Gravy:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, sugar, and salt.
  6. Add kasuri methi and tomato ketchup for enhanced flavor.
  7. Pour in fresh cream and mix well for a creamy texture.
  8. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
