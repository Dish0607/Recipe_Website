Fish Curry

Fish Curry is a flavorful and spicy dish made with fresh fish, aromatic spices, and a tangy tamarind base.

Ingredients

For the Curry:

  • 500g fish fillets (preferably white fish)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Curry leaves & fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and tamarind paste. Mix well and let the curry simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Gently add fish fillets to the curry and cook on low heat for 10-12 minutes until the fish is tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander and curry leaves.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
