Daal Makhni

Daal Makhni is a rich and creamy Punjabi lentil dish made with black lentils and kidney beans cooked with butter, cream, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

For the Daal:

  • 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste

For the Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak black lentils and kidney beans overnight. Drain and rinse.
  2. Pressure cook with water, turmeric, and salt for 4-5 whistles until soft.
  3. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  7. Mix in red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  8. Add cooked lentils and beans. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Stir in cream, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
