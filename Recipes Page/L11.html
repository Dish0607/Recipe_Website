Chole Poori

Chole Poori is a delicious North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (poori).

Ingredients

For the Chole (Chickpea Curry):

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cups canned chickpeas)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For the Poori:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

Preparing the Chole:

  1. Soak dried chickpeas overnight and pressure cook for 4-5 whistles until soft. If using canned chickpeas, drain and rinse well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, chole masala, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  6. Add the cooked chickpeas and some water to adjust consistency. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and lemon juice. Mix well.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Preparing the Poori:

  1. In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour, salt, and oil. Gradually add water and knead into a firm dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into small circles.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry each poori until golden brown and puffed.
  4. Drain excess oil and serve hot with chole.
