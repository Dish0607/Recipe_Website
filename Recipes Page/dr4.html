Lassi

Lassi is a traditional Indian yogurt-based drink that is refreshing and delicious. Follow this simple recipe to make a creamy and flavorful lassi at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 1/2 cup chilled water or milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • Chopped nuts (optional, for garnish)
  • Saffron strands (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Gather all the ingredients and ensure the yogurt is fresh and thick for a creamy texture.
  2. In a blender, combine the thick yogurt, chilled water or milk, sugar, cardamom powder, and ice cubes.
  3. Blend the ingredients on medium speed for 30-40 seconds or until the mixture is smooth, creamy, and frothy.
  4. Taste the lassi and adjust the sweetness or consistency by adding more sugar or a little extra milk/water as needed. Blend again if required.
  5. Pour the lassi into serving glasses. For an authentic touch, garnish with chopped nuts like almonds or pistachios and a few saffron strands.
  6. Serve the lassi immediately while it's fresh and cold. Enjoy your homemade lassi as a refreshing drink!
