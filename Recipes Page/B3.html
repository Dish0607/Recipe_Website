Khichdi

Khichdi is a nutritious and comforting Indian dish made with rice and lentils, cooked together with mild spices. It is easy to digest and perfect for a light meal.

Ingredients

For the Khichdi:

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal (yellow lentils)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

For the Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili (chopped, optional)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • A few curry leaves (optional)
  • 1 small carrot (chopped, optional)
  • 1/4 cup green peas (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and moong dal thoroughly under running water.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the rice, moong dal, water, turmeric powder, and salt.
  3. Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles until soft and mushy.
  4. In a separate pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds, chopped chili, grated ginger, and curry leaves.
  5. Once aromatic, add chopped carrots, peas, and black pepper powder. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Pour the tempering over the cooked rice and dal mixture. Mix well.
  7. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed, and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or papad.
