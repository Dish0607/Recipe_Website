Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate is a rich, creamy, and comforting drink perfect for chilly days. Follow this simple recipe to make a delicious homemade hot chocolate!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk (whole or any preferred type)
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate (chopped or chocolate chips)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional, for topping)
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate shavings (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, warm the milk until it is hot but not boiling.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Slowly whisk the cocoa mixture into the hot milk until fully dissolved and smooth.
  4. Stir in the chopped dark chocolate or chocolate chips and continue stirring until melted and well combined. Add the vanilla extract.
  5. Let the mixture simmer for a minute while stirring continuously to ensure a creamy texture.
  6. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs.
  7. Optionally, top with whipped cream or marshmallows and sprinkle cocoa powder or chocolate shavings for extra flavor.
  8. Enjoy your homemade hot chocolate while it's warm and comforting!
