Masala Tea

Masala tea, or chai, is a flavorful Indian spiced tea made with a blend of aromatic spices and milk. Follow this recipe to make a warm and soothing cup of masala chai!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 green cardamom pods (crushed)
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 small piece of ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns (crushed)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, add water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add crushed cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, and black peppercorns. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes to release the flavors.
  3. Add black tea leaves and let it steep for another 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the milk and sugar. Stir well and let it simmer for 2-3 more minutes.
  5. Strain the tea into cups using a fine sieve.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade masala tea!
