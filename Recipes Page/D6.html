Chocolate Brownies

These homemade chocolate brownies are rich, fudgy, and full of deep chocolate flavor. Perfect for dessert or a special treat!

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan with butter or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan or microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter completely. Allow it to cool slightly.
  3. Whisk in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until the mixture becomes smooth and glossy.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the cocoa powder, all-purpose flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring gently with a spatula or whisk until well combined. Do not overmix.
  6. If using, fold in the chocolate chips for an extra fudgy texture.
  7. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  9. Remove the pan from the oven and let the brownies cool in the pan for at least 10-15 minutes.
  10. Carefully lift the brownies out of the pan using the parchment paper (if used) and transfer them to a cutting board.
  11. Cut the brownies into squares or rectangles and serve. Enjoy your rich, fudgy homemade brownies!
