Gajar Ka Halwa

Gajar Ka Halwa, or Carrot Halwa, is a classic Indian dessert made with grated carrots, milk, sugar, and ghee. It's a winter favorite and is often prepared during festivals and celebrations.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 4 cups grated carrots
  • 2 cups full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 10-12 almonds
  • 2 tbsp raisins

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan and fry cashews, almonds, and raisins until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. Add the grated carrots to the pan and sauté for 5-7 minutes until they soften.
  3. Pour in the milk and mix well. Let it simmer on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the milk reduces and thickens.
  4. Once the milk has mostly evaporated, add the sugar and continue stirring. The mixture will loosen initially but will thicken again.
  5. Add cardamom powder and mix well to enhance the flavor.
  6. Continue cooking until the halwa starts leaving the sides of the pan and reaches the desired consistency.
  7. Finally, mix in the fried nuts and raisins.
  8. Serve warm or cold, garnished with extra nuts. Enjoy!
